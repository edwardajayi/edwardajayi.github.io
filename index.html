About

I am a Master's student in Engineering AI at Carnegie Mellon University Africa and a Mastercard Foundation Scholar. Currently, I work as a research assistant under Prof. Prasenjit Mitra at the Spatial and Language Technologies Lab at CMU Africa. My research interests lie in Socially Aware AI, Multimodal AI, Humor Generation and Detection, and AI for Mental Health.
